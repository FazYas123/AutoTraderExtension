Privacy Policy for AutoTrader Multi-Make Search

Information Collection and Use

The AutoTrader Multi-Make Search extension is designed with your privacy in mind. We prioritize the protection of your personal information and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

Data Storage

This extension stores only the car makes you select through the extension interface. This data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

What We Store

  • Your selected car makes (e.g., "BMW", "Audi", "Toyota")

How We Use Your Data

The stored car make selections are used solely to enhance your browsing experience on AutoTrader.co.uk by allowing you to search for multiple car makes simultaneously. This functionality modifies AutoTrader search API requests to include all your selected car makes.

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Share or sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or analytics
  • Store your data on external servers

Chrome API Usage

This extension adheres to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We only use Chrome APIs for their intended purpose and request only the minimum necessary permissions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date of this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
